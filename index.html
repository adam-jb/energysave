Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need solar panels?

No. The system works entirely from the grid — it charges from cheap off-peak electricity and discharges during expensive peak periods. If you have solar panels, the battery works with those too.

What electricity tariff do I need?

You'll need a time-of-use tariff like Octopus Agile, which prices electricity in 30-minute windows. This creates the price gap the battery exploits. Switching is free and takes minutes.

What's the warranty?

10 years, covering the battery, inverter, and installation workmanship. If anything fails within the warranty period, we repair or replace it at no cost.

Is it safe?

The battery uses lithium iron phosphate (LFP) — the safest type of lithium battery. Installation complies with PAS 63100:2024, the UK fire safety standard for home battery storage. All installations are registered with your local DNO and carried out by certified installers.

How long does installation take?

A single day. Our certified installers handle everything including DNO notification and all paperwork. Minimal disruption.

Will it work during a power cut?

The standard configuration is grid-tied and won't power your home during an outage (for safety — to protect grid engineers). Backup power capability is available as an optional upgrade.

Why is this so much cheaper than other installers?

This is an introductory offer to build our installation base quickly. We source batteries direct and keep overheads low. The price includes everything — there are no hidden costs or extras.

Do I own the battery?

Yes. You pay £3,499, you own it. No subscription, no revenue share, no ongoing payments. The 10-year warranty is included in the price.
